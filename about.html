What is Recall?

What is our game about?

Recall is meant to be a story based point and click game. In the game, you try to find your way in the mysterious version of the planet you know as earth.

Something does not seem right however...the world as you know it seems different

Gameplay

How does it work?

The game is a point and click story.

You can interact with entrances to other rooms in order to enter that room


You may sometimes find an item in the rooms that stands out more than others.

Clicking on these items can lead to some interesting outcomes...
