Entry Log #1

This is Dr. Raymond White,

The world as we know it has changed, families killed, cities toppled, the military scattered, all fallen to these horrible monstrosities, code name: Le'iosia. I have gathered up what I can of the remaining staff and supplies and have retreated to the company emergency laboratory deep in the woods where we should be safe from them. Our goal, stay hidden and work on some sort of method to fight back against these monsters. A pathogen, a weapon, anything will do, they will pay for what they have done to everyone, all of us here are as determined as the next to see that they do. But until then, we need to recuperate, gather up our strength and start working on a plan.
